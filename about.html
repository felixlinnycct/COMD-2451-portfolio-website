Hi There!

My name is Felix. I'm a college student at New York City College of Technology majoring in Communication Design with a passion for creativity and storytelling. My dream is to become a graphic designer, blending my love for visual aesthetics with innovative problem-solving. Whether it’s designing sleek layouts, creating bold logos, or crafting captivating visuals, I’m all about using design to communicate ideas that inspire and connect. When I’m not buried in projects or brainstorming concepts, you’ll probably find me watching movies, doodling in my sketchbook, or diving into a good playlist to fuel my creativity.
